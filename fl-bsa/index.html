Fair-Lending Bias-Simulation Appliance

FL‑BSA

Self‑hosted bias simulation that generates synthetic borrower cohorts (with loan decisions) to measure disparate impact and produce regulator‑ready evidence — with no customer data leaving your VPC.

Dual-Branch Analysis

FL-BSA separates amplification bias from intrinsic bias to isolate model-induced disparate impact.

The delta between branches isolates model-induced bias from pre-existing data patterns.

How it Works

1

Upload data

Upload structured data or summary statistics. FL-BSA establishes amplification vs. intrinsic baselines.

2

Calibrate branches

Synthesize borrower records with embedded decisions. Evaluate AIF360/Fairlearn metrics (DI, EO, TPR gaps).

3

Produce evidence

Export the signed manifest, PDF, and certificates. Artifacts chain task ID to model seeds to outputs for auditability.

4

Review and share

Share the evidence bundle with Compliance, Model Risk, and Internal Audit. Optional advisory runbooks available.

FAQ

Do we send any data to Equilens?

No. FL-BSA is self-hosted; all compute happens in your VPC/VM. Zero outbound calls by default.

How long does a typical run take?

From minutes for 10k rows to approximately 12 minutes for around 1M rows on validated hardware profiles; daily monitoring runs reuse the generator and complete in ≤5 minutes.

What regulatory frameworks are covered?

Evidence maps to ECOA/Reg B (US), EU AI Act, and FCA Consumer Duty. Certificates include Data Quality, Model Fidelity, Training Convergence, Synthetic Quality, and Regulatory Alignment.

What are the deployment options?

AWS Marketplace AMI or containerized via docker-compose. CPU-only and GPU-preferred profiles supported.

What's in the evidence bundle?

A 30-page PDF report, a signed manifest (dataset hash, RNG seed, software version), and certificates. Artifacts are chained for auditability and can be retrieved by Task ID.
