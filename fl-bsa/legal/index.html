FL-BSA Product Legal Summary

Snapshot of product-level responsibilities, licensing, and compliance artifacts. Company-wide policies remain at /legal/.

Vendor identity

FL-BSA is supplied by Valfox Ltd (trading as “Equilens”). Company number 14469638. Registered office: 840 Ibis Court, Centre Park, Warrington, Cheshire, England, WA1 1RL.

For corporate policies (privacy, terms, accessibility, export), see the Equilens legal hub.

Product scope

  • Self-hosted appliance delivered as AWS Marketplace AMI or docker-compose bundle.
  • No personal data is transmitted to Equilens by default; customers control telemetry.
  • Evidence bundle: PDF report, signed manifest (SHA-256), certificate appendix.

Licensing & usage

Usage terms are provided via AWS Marketplace subscription or Private Offer. Custom SLAs and professional services statements of work are executed separately.

Open-source components and notices are catalogued at /legal/open-source.html. SBOM and NOTICE excerpts are available on request.

Controls & attestations

  • Control mapping: FCA, EU AI Act, ECOA touchpoints documented in the evidence bundle.
  • Security posture: hardened images, optional Prometheus metrics; no unsolicited network egress.
  • Change management: versioned releases; manifest includes software build identifiers.

Engagement process

Start with the procurement path to review AWS Marketplace terms, deployment prerequisites, and evidence packages. For data processing agreements or custom clauses, email equilens@equilens.io.
