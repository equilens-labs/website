Procurement & Deployment

Subscribe via AWS Marketplace and deploy the self-hosted FL-BSA appliance in your VPC.

Deployment steps

1

Subscribe on AWS Marketplace

Open the AWS Marketplace listing and click Subscribe. Review and accept the standard terms.

2

Launch inside your account

Launch the container stack (Helm/docker-compose) in your account. Select instance type, region, VPC, and subnet per your change controls.

3

Boot the appliance

Boot runs docker-compose via systemd. The appliance listens on port 8080 (configurable), with optional Prometheus on 9090.

4

Run a test

Upload sample data or the FCA sandbox dataset. Produce the PDF report, signed manifest, and checksum log.

5

Move to Pilot

Activate the paid Pilot tier or request a Private Offer for enterprise terms once procurement approves.

Procurement package

Evidence bundle

  • Signed manifest (JSON) and PDF report with embedded hashes.
  • Infrastructure BOM and network diagram (request access).
  • Security attestations aligned to FCA, EU AI Act, and ECOA checkpoints.

Public synthetic/demo-only examples (release v4.0.3): customer_report.pdf, manifest.json, SHA256SUMS.txt.

Commercials

  • AWS Marketplace listing with Annual and multi-year licensing options.
  • Pilot tier (3-month) for regulated sandbox evaluations.
  • Private Offer support for custom terms or multi-entity rollouts.

Deployment options

Container (VM)

docker-compose stack (API, Worker, Redis). CPU-only profile available; GPU preferred for faster synthetic generation.

Alternate: AWS AMI

Single-tenant AMI with deterministic boot. Delivers API, worker, and reporting services; integrates with customer IAM.

Coordinate CSP, HSTS, and CAA updates through your network and DNS teams. We provide implementation runbooks on request.
