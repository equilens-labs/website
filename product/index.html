Why self-hosted?

  • Bank procurement prefers sealed appliances with clear data boundaries.
  • Evidence chains need deterministic manifests (hashes, seeds, configuration).
  • Regulators expect reproducible metrics aligned with ECOA, EU AI Act, FCA guidance.
