Canonical website icon generator backend: ImageMagick (`magick`) only.

Rationale:
- Prior sips/magick fallback produced byte-level drift across environments.
- Deterministic evidence and checksum workflows require a single rendering backend.

Scope:
- brand/icons/apple-touch-icon-180.png
- brand/icons/icon-192-maskable.png
- brand/icons/icon-512-maskable.png
- brand/icons/favicon.ico
