Data Processing Agreement (DPA) Position

The Equilens FL-BSA appliance runs entirely in customers’ environments. Equilens does not receive or process customer production data by default. We therefore are not acting as a processor for customer bias-testing data when you deploy FL-BSA in your own infrastructure.

If a customer requires Equilens to handle personal data (for example, during professional services), a separate data processing agreement will be provided on request. Contact equilens@equilens.io to initiate that process.
