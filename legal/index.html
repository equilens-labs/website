DPA Position

Posture statement

We provide a self-hosted software product (FL-BSA). We do not receive or process your production datasets in our systems when you deploy the product in your own environment.

Roles

Controller: We act as controller for website enquiries, support mailboxes, sales communications, and vendor management.

Processor: We act as processor only if you ask us to handle support materials that include personal data (e.g., logs, dumps) or if you enable telemetry to us (off by default).

DPA triggers

We will sign our DPA when we process customer personal data as a processor (support logs/telemetry/pilots). No DPA is required for self-hosted use where no customer personal data is sent to us.

Contact equilens@equilens.io to request a DPA or initiate that process.

Responsible Use

Prohibited uses

You must not use FL-BSA for:

  • Unlawful discrimination or contravention of fair-lending/EEA equality laws
  • Attempts to evade law or regulation
  • Use on unlawfully obtained datasets
  • Uses that violate export or sanctions laws
  • High-risk automated decisioning without appropriate human oversight

Customer responsibility

FL-BSA runs entirely within your environment and generates synthetic data for fairness testing. You remain responsible for regulatory compliance, dataset rights, model governance, and human review. Outputs are interpreted by qualified personnel.

Research vs production

Demo and evaluation outputs are not production approvals. Do not use FL-BSA to make production lending decisions without human review and appropriate governance controls.
