Accessibility

Accessibility statement for the public website equilens.io.

For the full legal hub, see /legal/.

We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and make reasonable adjustments in line with the UK Equality Act 2010.

If you encounter any barriers or need the information in an alternative format, email equilens@equilens.io. We review and address accessibility requests as quickly as possible.
