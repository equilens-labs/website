Responsible Use

Responsible-use boundaries for FL-BSA.

Prohibited uses

You must not use FL-BSA for:

  • Unlawful discrimination or contravention of fair-lending/EEA equality laws
  • Attempts to evade law or regulation
  • Use on unlawfully obtained datasets
  • Uses that violate export or sanctions laws
  • High-risk automated decisioning without appropriate human oversight

Customer responsibility

FL-BSA runs entirely within your environment and generates synthetic data for fairness testing. You remain responsible for regulatory compliance, dataset rights, model governance, and human review. Outputs are interpreted by qualified personnel.

Research vs production

Demo and evaluation outputs are not production approvals. Do not use FL-BSA to make production lending decisions without human review and appropriate governance controls.
