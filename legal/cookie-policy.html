Summary

We do not use analytics, advertising, or social media cookies. Pages are static and no third-party scripts run.

Strictly necessary operation

Our hosting provider may use infrastructure-level caching or logs to deliver the site reliably. These mechanisms fall under the UK PECR exemption for strictly necessary storage/access and are not used for profiling.

Future changes

If we introduce functionality that requires cookies or similar technologies (for example, analytics or authenticated docs), we will update this policy and deploy appropriate consent controls before enabling them.
