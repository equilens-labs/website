Self-hosted

Runs in your AWS VPC or on-premise VM. No data leaves your infrastructure. You control telemetry.

Evidence bundle

30-page PDF report, signed manifest (SHA-256), and certificates. Artefacts chain task ID → model seeds → outputs for audit trails.

Repeatable analysis

Systematic fairness testing, not ad-hoc. Daily monitoring runs complete in under 5 minutes. Same methodology every time.

Built for model governance teams

FL-BSA is designed for teams that own model risk and fair-lending compliance:

  • Model Risk / Model Validation
  • Compliance and Conduct Risk
  • Credit Risk and Portfolio Risk
  • Internal Audit (model governance)

If you explain model behaviour to regulators or audit committees, FL-BSA gives you concrete evidence to plug into your existing processes.
